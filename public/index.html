About Us

We are a luxury homestay located in the heart of the city.

Being just 9 minutes away from the Airport and 10 minutes from the Railway station, Pinnacle Homestay is the perfect place for you to stay in Bhubaneswar.

When you book with us, you don't just book a room, you book an experience. Our rooms are fully furnished with cozy beds, lights that help you work while being relaxed and television that keeps you entertained non-stop. Our staff are available 24x7 at your service and help you with all your needs. Your comfort and convenience is our priority! Amenities such as free Wi-Fi and conference rooms helps to work easily on the move while our luxury dining restaurant offers a lavish menu to keep you pampered. Transfer services eliminates the hassle of arranging a cab and worrying about your safety as our highly professional drivers make your travel a pleasant experience

Located near offices such as OCAC, Odisha Secretariat, Governor's House, Infocity and other major offices helps you save time travelling for work. Being just 14 minutes from the Khandagiri caves and 16 minutes from the famous Lingaraj Temple makes it a perfect place for travellers who want to experience the best of Bhubaneswar.

Call us today to book a home away from home!
