About Us

We are a luxury homestay located in the heart of the city.

Being just 9 minutes away from the Airport and 10 minutes from the Railway station, Pinnacle Homestay is the perfect place for you to stay in Bhubaneswar.

When you book with us, you don't just book a room, you book a stay-at-home like experience. Our rooms are fully furnished with cozy beds, lights that help you work while being relaxed and television that keeps you entertained non-stop. Our staff are available 24x7 at your service and help you with all your needs. Your comfort and convenience is our priority! Amenities such as free Wi-Fi and well-furnished lounge space help you to work homely on the move and also enable you to enjoy small meetings or family gatherings. Our luxury dining restaurant offers a lavish menu to keep you pampered. Transfer services eliminates the hassle of arranging a cab and worrying about your safety as our highly professional drivers make your travel a pleasant experience

Offices nearby: OCAC, OMC, Rabindra Mandap, Passport Office, Odisha Secretariat, Governor's House, Infocity and other major offices.
